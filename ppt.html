Introduction

Medication non-adherence remains a critical healthcare challenge, affecting approximately 50% of elderly patients. Our Smart Pill Management System addresses this by automating the medication dispensing process using IoT technologies and advanced sensor integration. The system combines ESP32 microcontroller capabilities with precision sensors including load cells (HX711), color sensors, and real-time clocks (RTC) to ensure accurate and timely medication delivery. By incorporating weight-based dose verification and color sensor-based pill identification, the system eliminates human errors in medication administration. The dual verification mechanism ensures that patients receive the correct medication in the correct dosage at the right time. Integration with SD card storage enables comprehensive logging of all dispensing events, facilitating adherence tracking and clinical audit. The servo motor-controlled dispensing mechanism provides precise control over medication quantity, while the IoT connectivity allows healthcare providers to monitor medication adherence remotely and receive alerts for missed doses. This solution is particularly beneficial for elderly patients, chronically ill individuals, and care facilities where medication management is critical for patient outcomes.
